Who's using our product?

Knit Hat Desginer is a hat pattern design application for all knitters with a basic skill level. Our app was built with the practical knitter in mind. Why continue to buy single use patterns that are designed for a single gauge of yarn? We think that, with a little help from a computer, normal people should be able to design simple hat patterns by themselves. Knit Hat Designer helps you to do exactly that!
